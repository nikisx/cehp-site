Who We Are

Center for European Health Policies (CEHP) is a consulting company focused on medical devices, IVDs, health technology assessment, and Medtech innovation across Europe.

Our Mission

To empower healthcare organizations and MedTech companies with expert consulting in medical devices regulation, clinical performance evaluation, and health technology assessment, ensuring safe and effective products reach European markets.

Our Focus

We specialize in MDR/IVDR compliance, CE marking, clinical trials for medical devices, conformity assessment, technical dossier preparation, secondary manufacturing, and market access strategies for MedDevs and IVDs in the European Union.

Our Vision

To be the leading European consulting partner for health technology assessment and medical device regulatory affairs, driving innovation and patient access through data-driven insights and deep industry expertise in the Medtech ecosystem.

#Our Team

Expert Teams Driving Results

Our multidisciplinary team combines academic excellence with deep industry experience to deliver comprehensive solutions across the full MedTech value chain.

Consultancy

Expert regulatory consultants providing strategic guidance on MDR/IVDR compliance, CE marking, clinical evaluation, and market access for medical devices and IVDs across European markets.

Strategy Development and Project Creating Specialists

Dedicated professionals designing tailored strategies for medical device companies, from regulatory pathway planning and HTA submissions to clinical development programs and manufacturing optimization.

Regulatory, Clinical and Market Access Teams

Specialized teams covering the complete regulatory lifecycle: from clinical investigation design and technical dossier preparation to conformity assessment and post-market surveillance, ensuring seamless market access.
