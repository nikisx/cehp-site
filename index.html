#Welcome message

Prof. Dr. Albena Zlatareva, MD, PhD

CEO

"The European healthcare market can be considered a component of national security, particularly in middle-income countries. An aging population, a severe demographic crisis, a higher prevalence of chronic diseases, and rising healthcare costs define it. At the same time, there is a strong push toward a patient-centric system driven by rapid advancements in medical technology and an increasingly restrictive regulatory environment. To navigate these challenges effectively, high-quality expertise and services that integrate these critical aspects will support strategic decision-making and successful market implementation."
